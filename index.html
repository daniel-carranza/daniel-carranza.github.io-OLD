My CV

I am an MSc student in mathematics at the University of Toronto interested in homotopy theory and (higher) category theory.

In 2022-2023, I will be a Program Associate at the MSRI. Starting Fall 2023, I will be a PhD student at Johns Hopkins University.

Previously, I was an undergradute student at the University of Western Ontario under the supervision of Chris Kapulkin.
